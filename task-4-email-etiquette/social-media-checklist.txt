Do's (Recommended)
	Don'ts (Avoid)
	Keep it Private: Set your personal accounts (Instagram/Snapchat) to private to control who sees your life.
	Overshare Info: Never post your home address, phone number, or photos of your College ID/Admit card.
	Professional Branding: Use a clean, recognizable headshot for your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles.
	Engage in Trolling: Avoid online arguments or leaving rude comments on public posts.
	Fact-Check First: Verify news or links before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.
	Post Sensitive Content: Don't post photos involving illegal activities or anything that violates college policy.
	Network Smartly: Follow industry leaders and companies you want to work for in the future.
	Ignore Privacy Settings: Don't leave your "Tagged" photos unreviewed; untag yourself from unprofessional posts.
	Think Before Posting: Ask yourself: "Would I be okay with a future employer seeing this post?"
	Share OTPs/Logins: Never share your digital credentials or bank OTPs, even in "private" DMs.
