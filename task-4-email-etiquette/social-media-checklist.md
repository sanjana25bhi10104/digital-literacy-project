**Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students**

| Do's (Responsible Use) | Don'ts (Risky Behavior) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Set Profiles to Private:** Keep personal accounts (Instagram/Snapchat) restricted to people you actually know. | **Share Personal Details:** Never post your home address, phone number, or photos of your college ID/Admit card. |
| **Build a Professional Brand:** Use a clear, professional headshot for your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles. | **Engage in Online Conflicts:** Avoid "trolling" or leaving rude, offensive comments on public posts. |
| **Fact-Check Before Sharing:** Always verify news or links before reposting them to avoid spreading fake news. | **Post Sensitive Content:** Don't post photos involving illegal activities or anything that violates college policy. |
| **Audit Your Tagged Photos:** Regularly check and untag yourself from unprofessional photos posted by others. | **Share Credentials:** Never share your passwords, bank OTPs, or ERP logins, even in private messages. |
| **Think Before You Post:** Ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future recruiter seeing your post. | **Ignore Privacy Updates:** Don't forget to review your security settings every few months as platforms change. |
